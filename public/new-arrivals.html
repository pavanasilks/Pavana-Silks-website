Handloomed with unparalleled precision, this Kanchipuram masterpiece features pure golden zari borders and classic motif weaves. The deep, rich texture of our premium silk and the brilliance of traditional gold brocade make it a true heirloom treasure, fresh from the loom.

Weave Craft Korvai Handloom Weaving

Enquire via WhatsApp

This saree is made from 100% pure Mulberry silk, double-warped for a full-bodied texture. To maintain its natural sheen and gold embroidery quality: dry clean only, avoid direct sunlight when storing, and store wrapped in light muslin cloth.

Korvai is a traditional south Indian handloom weaving technique where the body and the border are woven separately and then joined together by a skilled artisan using a locking pattern. The process is labor-intensive and results in borders that do not slip or tear.
